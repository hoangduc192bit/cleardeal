# Arc brand usage in ClearDeal

Last reviewed: 2026-07-19

Official source: https://www.arc.io/brand-guidelines-and-partner-toolkit

## Positioning

ClearDeal is an independent product built on Arc. Arc is the settlement infrastructure.

Approved descriptive language includes:

- ClearDeal is built on Arc.
- ClearDeal supports Arc Testnet.
- ClearDeal is available on Arc Testnet.

Do not imply that Circle created, operates, endorses, or formally partners with ClearDeal unless that relationship has been approved in writing.

## Identity and logo

The ClearDeal name and visual identity must remain primary. Do not incorporate Arc into the product name, app icon, or logo system. Use only current Arc assets from the official Circle Brand Kit, and do not modify, recolor, distort, or recreate them.

## Network claims

Use `Arc Testnet` for the current deployment environment. Do not imply Arc mainnet availability or real-world value for testnet USDC.

## Required attribution

Where feasible, state:

> Arc is a trademark of Circle Internet Group, Inc. and/or its affiliates. Use of the Arc name describes network support and does not imply endorsement.
